The Podcast

Who We Are

BRDO is the podcast where preparation is optional but opinions are mandatory. Two mates having the kind of conversations that start with "I don't really know much about this, but—" and end up somewhere surprisingly interesting.

Every week we pick a topic, barely scratch the surface of the facts, and dive deep into what we actually think about it. No expert credentials. No research notes. Just two people confident enough in their takes to hit record.
